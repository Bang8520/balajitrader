Balaji Trades

Distributor of Numaligarh Refinery Limited (NRL) Government of India Enterprise

We Balaji Traders (Proprietor Suresh Bang) established in 2001, distributor of Numaligarh Refinery Limited (NRL) Government of India Enterprise is one of the leading importers and suppliers of industrial chemicals and waxes, which are known for their excellent quality and performance in the market. Our company deals in a wide and unmatched range of products which includes Industrial Wax, PVC Wax, Polyethylene Waxes, Semi Refined Paraffin Wax, Zinc Oxide/Stearic Acid, Gum Rosin, Microcrystalline Wax, Fully Refined Paraffin Wax, White Petroleum Jelly, Candle Raw Material, Stearic Acid Powder, Microcrystalline, Residue Wax, Slack Wax, Carnauba Wax etc. We also have the flexibility to improve our range and introduce new products.

Our physical presence is in Mumbai and Ahmedabad. Committed to delivering high-quality industrial chemicals and waxes at an economical price, we have gained wide acceptance from our clients in the domestic and international markets.
